Smarter Code, Faster Teams

BugHawk was born from a simple idea: what if AI could be a true partner in the software development lifecycle? This project, built by students from VIT, Pune, aims to create that partner.

What is BugHawk?

BugHawk isn't just another linter. It's an intelligent copilot designed to analyze Git repositories for a wide spectrum of issues. We leverage AI to go beyond simple syntax and find deep-seated problems:

  • Security Vulnerabilities: Finding risks like SQLi and XSS before they're exploited.
  • Performance Bottlenecks: Identifying inefficient code that slows your app down.
  • Predictive Analysis: Using historical data to predict sensitive parts of your code.
  • Automated Documentation: Generating clear documentation so your team can stay in sync.

Our goal is to provide a single "Project Health Score" that gives development teams an at-a-glance understanding of their codebase's quality, allowing them to ship flawless code, faster.
